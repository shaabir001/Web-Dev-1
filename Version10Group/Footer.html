Footer

A footer refers to the area located at the bottom of a webpage or document, usually encompassing essential information such as contact information, copyright statements, and links to privacy policies. It functions as an effective navigational aid, granting users swift access to vital resources while maintaining a clean main content area. In web design, footers are frequently designed with a minimalist approach to prevent distraction from the core content. Additionally, they may incorporate social media icons, site map links, or legal disclaimers to enrich the user experience. The footer plays a significant role in offering supplementary context and enhancing site usability without inundating the reader.
